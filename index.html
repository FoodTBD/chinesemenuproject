About

Every menu from a restaurant provides a snapshot of a diaspora in time and space, both in terms of culinary and linguistic evolution. Our goal is to build a massive dataset of this knowledge and make it freely accessible for a variety of purposes, from data science and machine learning to humanities research and artistic projects.
