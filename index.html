The Chinese Restaurant Menu Collection Project

Collecting photos of Chinese restaurants menus from around the world.

About

Every menu from a Chinese restaurant offers a snapshot of the evolution of not only a global cuisine but also the vast diaspora of a world language. Our goal is to build a massive dataset of this culinary knowledge and make it freely accessible for a variety of purposes, from data science and machine learning to humanities research and artistic projects.
